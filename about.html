Our Story

ZelCon Enterprise was founded with a simple mission: to provide homeowners and businesses with roofing solutions they can trust. What started as a small team of dedicated roofers has grown into one of the most respected roofing companies in the region, known for quality workmanship and customer satisfaction.

  • Licensed and certified roofing professionals with extensive training
  • Commitment to using only premium, weather-resistant materials
  • Dedication to safety, quality, and customer satisfaction on every project

Over the years, we've built our reputation one roof at a time, earning the trust of countless satisfied customers. Our team combines traditional roofing expertise with modern techniques and materials to deliver roofing systems that protect your property for decades to come.
